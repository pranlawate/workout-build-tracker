# Exercise Rotation System - User Guide

## Overview

The rotation system ensures you develop complete muscle coverage by automatically suggesting exercise variations every 8-12 weeks.

## How It Works

### Automatic Suggestions

After training an exercise for 8 weeks, you'll see a rotation suggestion:

```
⟳ Try Standard DB Curls for complete bicep coverage (8 weeks on current variation)
[Switch Exercise]
```

**Why 8 weeks?**
- Muscle adaptation peaks at 6-8 weeks
- Rotating prevents adaptive plateau
- Ensures all muscle heads get trained

### Manual Rotation

You can rotate anytime by:
1. Click exercise name in workout
2. Select different variation from dropdown
3. Tenure resets automatically

### Rotation Pools

**Mandatory rotations** (missing muscle heads):
- **DB Hammer Curls ↔ Standard DB Curls**
  - Hammer: brachialis + brachioradialis
  - Standard: biceps long + short heads

- **Tricep Pushdowns ↔ Overhead Tricep Extension**
  - Pushdowns: lateral + long heads
  - Overhead: long + medial heads

**Optional rotations** (barbell compound variations):
- Bench Press ↔ Close-Grip Bench
- Back Squat ↔ Front Squat
- Deadlift ↔ Sumo Deadlift
- More options unlock as you progress

## Unlock Proximity

**When you're close to unlocking** (80%+ toward milestone):
- Rotation suggestions pause
- Focus on hitting unlock milestone
- Prevents interrupting progress

Example: You're at 12.5kg × 12 reps on DB Hammer Curls (milestone: 15kg × 12)
- Progress: 83% (12.5 ÷ 15)
- Rotation suppressed - keep pushing toward unlock!

## Rotation-Based Unlocks

**To unlock the next tier**, you must:
1. Hit milestone on current variation (e.g., 15kg × 12 on DB Hammer Curls)
2. Switch to rotation variant
3. Hit milestone AGAIN on rotation variant (e.g., 15kg × 12 on Standard DB Curls)
4. Unlock appears when BOTH variations reach milestone twice

**Why twice on each?**
- Proves movement pattern mastery
- Ensures you don't have muscle imbalances
- Verifies complete development before progression

## Rotation Strategy

**Early weeks (0-7 weeks):**
- Focus on progressive overload
- Learn the movement pattern
- Build strength foundation

**Rotation window (8-12 weeks):**
- Accept rotation suggestion when it appears
- Try the variation for a cycle
- Develop complete muscle coverage

**Unlock phase (80%+ progress):**
- Rotation suggestions pause
- Focus on hitting milestone
- Switch after unlocking if desired

## Benefits

- **Complete muscle development** - All muscle heads trained
- **Prevents adaptation** - Variation drives continued progress
- **Reduces injury risk** - Balanced development across muscle groups
- **Maintains interest** - Variety prevents boredom
- **Smart unlocks** - Proves mastery before advancing

## FAQ

**Q: Can I ignore rotation suggestions?**
A: Yes! They're suggestions. Manual exercise selection always available.

**Q: What if I prefer one variation?**
A: Totally fine! System tracks your choice. Suggestion reappears in 8 weeks.

**Q: Do I lose progress when rotating?**
A: No - weight history saved separately per exercise.

**Q: Can I rotate before 8 weeks?**
A: Absolutely! Click exercise name, select variation anytime.

**Q: What about exercises without rotation pools?**
A: No automatic suggestions. Just progressive overload on that movement.
