## Executive Summary

**Chosen Structure:** Upper/Lower Split (4-workout rotation)
- **Upper A:** Horizontal Push/Pull emphasis
- **Lower A:** Bilateral/Compound emphasis
- **Upper B:** Vertical Push/Pull emphasis
- **Lower B:** Unilateral/Accessory emphasis

**Key Features:**
- 6-8 exercises per session (down from current 10)
- Each muscle 2x/week frequency
- Can train consecutive days (Upper ‚Üí Lower safe)
- Flexible 2-6 days/week scheduling
- Addresses weaknesses: rotator cuff, lower back, hip abductors
